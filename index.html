The Tata Curvv is a coupe SUV that was first shown as a concept model in April 2022. The production version was launched on August 7, 2024.

Concept model

The Curvv EV concept was first shown in April 2022. The Curvv ICE's near-production concept was shown at Bharat Mobility 2023. The concept models introduced Tata's latest design philosophy.

Features

The Curvv has a panoramic sunroof, a free-standing touchscreen infotainment system, and a digital instrument cluster. It also has wireless CarPlay/Android Auto, a wireless charger, and ambient lighting.

Engine options

The Curvv has a 1.2-litre turbocharged petrol engine, a 1.2-litre direct injection turbocharged petrol engine, and a 1.5-litre turbo-diesel engine. The Curvv EV has a 45 kWh battery pack option and a 55 kWh battery pack option.
